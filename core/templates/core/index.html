Blood & Vaccine Facts

Lifesaver

A single blood donation can save up to three lives. Your contribution has a massive impact on the community.

Vaccines & Donation

Most vaccines (like COVID-19 or Flu) don't stop you from donating if you feel well. Stay protected and keep saving lives!

Universal Type

O-Negative is the universal donor type. It's vital for emergency rooms and trauma centers during critical hours.

Quick Recovery

Your body replaces blood plasma within 24-48 hours. Just stay hydrated and have a light snack after your donation!
